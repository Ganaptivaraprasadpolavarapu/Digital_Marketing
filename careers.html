Digital Marketing Career Paths

SEO Specialist
Improves visibility in search engines using organic tactics.
Skills: SEO, Google Search Console, Ahrefs
Avg Salary: ₹3–6 LPA
Content Marketer
Creates content to attract and engage target audiences.
Skills: Blogging, Content Strategy, Copywriting
Avg Salary: ₹3–5 LPA
Social Media Manager
Handles brand presence on social platforms.
Skills: Canva, Instagram Ads, Engagement
Avg Salary: ₹3–6 LPA
Email Marketing Specialist
Automates campaigns for customer engagement.
Skills: Mailchimp, Automation, A/B Testing
Avg Salary: ₹4–7 LPA
PPC Expert
Runs paid ads to drive targeted traffic.
Skills: Google Ads, Analytics, Optimization
Avg Salary: ₹5–8 LPA
Digital Marketing Analyst
Tracks marketing KPIs and advises improvement.
Skills: Google Analytics, Data Studio
Avg Salary: ₹6–9 LPA
Affiliate Marketer
Earns via commissions by promoting others' products.
Skills: SEO, Email Lists, Research
Avg Salary: ₹2–5 LPA (Varies)
Digital Marketing Manager
Leads team and plans the digital strategy.
Skills: Strategy, Leadership, ROI
Avg Salary: ₹8–15 LPA
